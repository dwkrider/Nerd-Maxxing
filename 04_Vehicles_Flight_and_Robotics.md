# Vehicles, Flight & Robotics

> *Everything that moves under its own power.*

| # | Badge | Achievement |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | The Internal Combustion | Tear down and successfully rebuild a small four-stroke engine to running condition. |
| 2 | The Rotor Tuner | Assemble a custom drone from components, solder the ESCs, and tune the PID control loops until it flies stable. |
| 3 | The Rocketeer | Design, build, and launch a model rocket with an altimeter payload that is recovered intact. |
| 4 | The Autonomous | Program a robot (wheeled, legged, or aerial) to navigate an obstacle course without human input using onboard sensors. |
| 5 | The Spoke Master | Hand-lace and true a bicycle wheel to within 1mm of radial and lateral runout. |
| 6 | The Linkage Crafter | Build a physical kinematic sculpture using a Klann or Jansen walking linkage that converts rotary motion to a smooth gait. |
| 7 | The Submariner | Build a functional ROV or submersible drone capable of operating underwater with live video feed. |
| 8 | The Wing Builder | Design and build an RC aircraft from raw materials (balsa, foam, or composite) that achieves sustained controlled flight. |
| 9 | The EV Surgeon | Diagnose and service a high-voltage component on a modern electric or hybrid vehicle — battery module, inverter, or charging system. |
| **FINAL BOSS** | **The Full-Stack Flyer** | **Design an airframe from scratch, fabricate it, integrate avionics and a flight controller, and fly it — autonomous or RC — on a mission profile you defined: survey, delivery, or data collection.** |
