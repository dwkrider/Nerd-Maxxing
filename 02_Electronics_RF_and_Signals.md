# Electronics, RF & Signals

> *Commanding the flow of electrons and the invisible spectrum.*

| # | Badge | Achievement |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | The Surgeon | Desolder and replace a blown surface-mount component on a motherboard, restoring it to function. |
| 2 | The Waverider | Use an oscilloscope to capture, measure, and interpret an analog signal from a live circuit. |
| 3 | The Crystal Receiver | Build a functional AM crystal radio from scratch that receives a broadcast station without external power. |
| 4 | The Antenna Smith | Design and build a directional antenna (Yagi-Uda or helical) for a specific frequency and measure its gain improvement. |
| 5 | The SDR Explorer | Use a Software Defined Radio to receive and decode real-world signals — ADS-B aircraft transponders, NOAA weather satellites, or pager traffic. |
| 6 | The Ham | Earn an amateur radio license and make a verified contact on HF, VHF, or UHF bands. |
| 7 | The Resurrector | Take a piece of dead vintage computing hardware (pre-2000), diagnose the fault to component level, and bring it back to life. |
| 8 | The Analog Alchemist | Build a voltage-controlled oscillator from discrete transistors and op-amps that produces a clean sine wave. |
| 9 | The Firmware Flasher | Write and flash custom firmware onto a microcontroller (ESP32, STM32, or AVR) that reads a sensor and serves data over a network. |
| **FINAL BOSS** | **The Silicon Architect** | **Design an original circuit from schematic, route a custom multi-layer PCB, order the boards, and hand-solder all surface-mount components into a fully working device.** |
