# Physics & Chemistry

> *The Natural Philosopher's laboratory — hands-on encounters with fundamental forces.*

| # | Badge | Achievement |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | The Spectroscopist | Build a spectrometer and correctly identify the emission lines of at least three different elements by their spectral fingerprints. |
| 2 | The Particle Hunter | Construct a Wilson Cloud Chamber and photograph the vapor trails of alpha particles, beta particles, and cosmic ray muons. |
| 3 | The High Voltage | Build a Tesla coil, Marx generator, or Van de Graaff generator capable of producing visible arcs in open air. |
| 4 | The Plasma Maker | Build a vacuum system, evacuate a chamber to at least 1 torr, and produce a stable visible glow discharge. |
| 5 | The Synthesist | Perform a multi-step chemical synthesis of a known compound and verify its identity and purity through melting point, spectroscopy, or chromatography. |
| 6 | The Biohacker | Extract DNA, perform a bacterial transformation or gel electrophoresis, and observe the results — hands-on molecular biology at a community biolab or home setup. |
| 7 | The Chladni Resonator | Drive a metal plate at resonance to produce visible Chladni figures, and map the nodal patterns across at least five distinct harmonic modes. |
| 8 | The Holographer | Produce a hologram using a laser, beam splitter, and photosensitive plate — a true interference-pattern recording of a three-dimensional object. |
| 9 | The Laser Builder | Build a functioning laser from discrete components — a nitrogen laser, a ruby laser, or a diode-pumped system — and characterize its output. |
| **FINAL BOSS** | **The Fusioneer** | **Build a Farnsworth fusor, achieve sustained plasma confinement with deuterium gas, and detect neutrons from deuterium-deuterium fusion reactions.** |
