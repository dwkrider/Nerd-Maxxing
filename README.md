# Nerd Maxxing: The Century of Badges

**100 nerd merit badges across 10 categories. No difficulty tiers. No participation trophies. Just creation and mastery.**

These are not badges for consuming nerd culture. They are badges for building, making, and understanding — from machining metal to proving theorems to pilgrimaging to Antarctica. To complete this list is to become a true polymath.

## Structure

- **10 categories**, each with **9 achievements + 1 Final Boss**
- Every Final Boss synthesizes its entire category into a single capstone project
- Complete all 10 Final Bosses to attempt **The Omni-Boss** — a single magnum opus spanning all disciplines

## The Omni-Boss: The Prime Architect

Design, fabricate, and deploy an autonomous electro-mechanical system that incorporates all 10 disciplines — custom-machined hardware, your own PCB, self-hosted AI, active control systems, mathematical algorithms, physical phenomena, field data collection, hand-bound documentation, historical depth, and installation at a site of significance.
